What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a decentralized peer to peer network protocol developed out of the Cypherpunks movement which implements a public digital ledger.

Bitcoin is often referred to as a cryptocurrency.

Bitcoin is a framework upon which efforts like the Ordinals Protocol can be built.
